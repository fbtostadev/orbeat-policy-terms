Privacy Policy — Orbeat

This page explains, in simple terms, how Orbeat handles information and what your rights are.

About Orbeat

Orbeat is a rhythm game. We prioritize a simple, offline experience. Ads are displayed to keep the game free.

What data do we collect

Orbeat does not collect personally identifiable information directly in the app.

We may display third-party ads. These services may process anonymous information (e.g., device identifiers, impression/click events) to personalize or measure ads, according to their own policies.

You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (e.g., Settings → Privacy → Advertising).

Why we use this information

  • To display ads and keep the game free.
  • To improve stability and performance (through aggregated third-party data).

Sharing

Orbeat does not share personally identifiable information. Third-party services (such as ad networks) may handle data according to their own policies.

Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights such as: access, correction, deletion, restriction of processing, portability, and withdrawal of consent. To exercise these, contact us via the email below.

Contact

Have questions about privacy? Write to orbeat.game@gmail.com. Average response time: 1–3 business days.

Policy updates

We may update this document to reflect game improvements or legal changes. The current version will always be published on this page.
