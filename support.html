Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Does Orbeat need internet to play?

No. The game works fully offline. Extra features like ads require a connection.

What kind of game is Orbeat?

It’s a rhythm game with a single track. The experience is about progression and increasing challenge in this one level.

Is there difficulty progression?

Yes. The single level grows harder as you progress, making the challenge evolve with your play.

Does Orbeat have in-app purchases or hidden costs?

The game is free to play and shows ads. Future updates may include other options, but there are no hidden costs.

What is the age rating?

Orbeat is rated for everyone.

Can I disable visual effects?

Yes. There’s an option to enable or disable VFX as you prefer.

Which languages are available?

Currently: Portuguese and English.

Quick Troubleshooting

  1. Close and reopen the app.
  2. Restart your device.
  3. Check if you have enough free storage.
  4. Make sure the app is updated to the latest version.
  5. If the issue continues, contact support.
